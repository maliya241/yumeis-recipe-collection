Cheddar Baked Chicken

Recipe Multiplier

1

Ingredients

  • 1/8 cup Butter *
  • 1/2 cup Flour *
  • 1 teaspoon Salt
  • 1 teaspoon Black Pepper
  • 1 teaspoon Garlic Powder
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 tablespoon Milk
  • 1 cup Cheddar Cheese, shredded
  • 1 cup Breadcrumbs *
  • 2 boneless skinless Chicken Breasts

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400℉.
  2. In a small mixing bowl, combine flour, salt, pepper, and garlic powder; set aside.
  3. In another bowl, whisk together eggs and milk; set aside.
  4. In a third bowl, combine cheddar cheese and breadcrumbs; set aside.
  5. Dip each chicken breast in the flour mixture, then the egg mixture and finish by coating it in the breadcrumb mixture.
  6. Place coated chicken breasts in the prepared pan and use the butter to put a pat of butter on top of each piece of chicken.
  7. Bake for 25 minutes, or until chicken is cooked through.

Note

  • * Changed from Original Recipe.
