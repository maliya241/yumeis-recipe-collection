No Bake Chocolate Oatmeal Cookies

Recipe Multiplier

1

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Granulated Sugar *
  • 4 tablespoons Margarine or Butter *
  • 1/4 cup Low-Fat Milk *
  • 1/6 cup Baking Cocoa *
  • 1 1/2 cups Quick or Old Fashioned Oats, uncooked *
  • 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract **

Directions

  1. In large saucepan, combine sugar, margarine, milk, and cocoa. Bring to boil over medium heat, stirring frequently.
  2. Continue boiling 3 minutes, stirring frequently.
  3. Remove from heat. Stir in oats. If using old fashioned oats, cool mixture in saucepan for 5 minutes.
  4. Drop by tablespoonfuls onto waxed paper.
  5. Let stand until firm. Store tightly covered.

Note

  • * Half the Original Recipe.
  • ** Added ingredient.
