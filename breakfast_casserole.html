Breakfast Casserole

Recipe Multiplier

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 oz) can Crescent Rolls *
  • 1/2 pound Ground Beef or Sausage *
  • 4 ounces Cream Cheese *

Directions

  1. Heat oven to 375℉.
  2. Unroll half of crescent roll can in an ungreased baking dish. Firmly press perforations to seal the dough.
  3. In a skillet, brown meat over medium heat, stirring frequently. Discard drippings. Add cream cheese to the same skillet, and mix together with the meat.
  4. Spoon cream cheese and meat mixture evenly over crust in baking dish.
  5. Use the rest of the crescent roll dough to cover the cream cheese and meat mixture.
  6. Bake 21 to 26 minutes or until golden brown.
  7. Cool 15 minutes. Cut into small squares.

Note

  • * Half of the Original Recipe
