Potato Cubes

Ingredients

  • Potatoes, Cubed
  • Vegetable Oil

Directions

  1. Cube the potatoes, and put the potatoes into a microwave safe bowl.
  2. Drizzle some oil on the potatoes and stir.
  3. Parcook the potatoes in the microwave by covering the bowl with a plate and cooking them in the microwave for about 6-7 minutes. Stir once about half-way through.
  4. Turn the stove on medium heat.
  5. Pour some oil and the potatoes into the pan.
  6. Cook until it becomes a desirable brown color.
