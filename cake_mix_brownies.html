Cake Mix Brownies

Recipe Multiplier

1

Ingredients

  • 1 box Cake Mix
  • 1/4 cup Vegetable Oil
  • 1/3 cup Milk
  • 1 large Egg
  • 1/2 bag Chocolate Chips
  • Spray On Cooking Oil (like Pam)

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350℉.
  2. Oil a 9x9 pan.
  3. Mix all ingredients with a strong spoon. It will be thick and very uncake-like.
  4. Smooth out the batter into the pan. It doesn't have to be perfectly flat.
  5. Bake for 15 minutes.
  6. Remove from heat and let cool.
  7. Cut into squares and serve with milk.
