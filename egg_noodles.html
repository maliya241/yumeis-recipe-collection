Homemade Egg Noodles

Recipe Multiplier

1

Ingredients

  • 1 large Egg, beaten
  • 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  • 2 tablespoons Milk
  • 1 cup All-Purpose Flour

Directions

  1. Combine egg(s), salt, and milk. Add flour. Stir until the dough mostly comes together.
  2. Lightly flour a clean work surface. Drop dough onto surface and form into a ball. Knead the dough for 5-10 minutes, or until the ingredients are fully incorporated and pliable.
  3. Roll out dough 1/8 inch thick. If your dough starts to stick gently lift up and add a bit more flour underneath.
  4. Using a pizza cutter, cut the dough in half widthwise and then into strips lengthwise. Make the strips about 1/4 inch thick. After cutting all the strips, make little piles of noodles and let them sit for a few minutes.
  5. Drop noodles into boiling water and cook for about 3 minutes.
