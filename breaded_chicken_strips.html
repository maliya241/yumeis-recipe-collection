Breaded Chicken Strips

Recipe Multiplier

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces Breadcrumbs
  • 1 cup All Purpose Flour
  • 2 large Eggs
  • 3 Boneless Skinless Chicken Breasts
  • Vegetable Oil

Directions

  1. Pour the flour (can add salt and pepper for flavoring), eggs (beaten), and breadcrumbs (can be seasoned with 3 tablespoons grated parmesan, 2 tablespoons minced fresh parsley leaves, 3/4 teaspoon kosher salt, and black pepper, or another seasoning) into their own separate shallow dishes.
  2. Pound the chicken breasts into even thickness.
  3. Cut the chicken into 1 1/2 inch strips. Then pat dry.
  4. Dip each strip into the flour, then shake off the excess.
  5. Next run the strips through the egg to coat it lightly and hold the chicken over the liquid to let any excess fall back into the bowl.
  6. Finally, lay the strips in the breadcrumbs, turn it over, and press it into the breading to evenly coat it.
  7. Heat 1/4 inch of oil in a large skillet over meduim heat until hot and shimmering.
  8. Lay 2 or 3 chicken strips at a time in the pan.
  9. Cook the chicken without turning until golden brown, about 2 minutes.
  10. Turn the chicken and cook evenly brown on the other side and the chicken is cooked through, about 2 to 3 minutes more.
